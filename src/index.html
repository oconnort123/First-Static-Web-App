ABOUT

Planet X aims to offer a partial solution by installing cheap and easy deployable carbon capture system to counteract CO2 emissions. Once your own carbon capture system is put in place you will earn "CO2s" (our own currency) with every cycle that is completed. You can spend your "CO2s" in our "market place".
